Eco-Friendly Brands


    1. Sand Cloud

    This eco-friendly brand has a mission to #savethefishes. In order for them to achieve this, they design their products with sustainability in mind. Their goal is the conserve our oceans. Their products range from clothing & towels to lotions and sunscreen while all bring eco-friendly.



    2. Toms

    Toms uses sustainable material to create their shoes. Toms also supports many sustainable companies and helps give back to people. A couple of example of the kind of companies they support are safe water supplies, safe birth services, and providing eyeglasses to people who can not afford them.



    3. Lush



    Lush is a handmade vegetarian bath & body brand. They create products meant for bath & body using all natural and organic ingredients. They are dedicated to staying green & helping the environment.
